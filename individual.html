Individual Therapy 18+ years

About

Individual therapy provides a confidential and supportive environment for adults (18 years and older) to explore their mental health challenges. This service is designed for clients facing a range of issues, including grief, mood regulation, stress, trauma, and anxiety. With a focus on evidence-based practices, therapy is tailored to meet each individual's unique needs and circumstances.

Incorporating expressive art therapy, clients engage in creative processes that facilitate emotional expression and insight. This therapeutic modality allows individuals to explore their feelings in a non-verbal way, promoting self-discovery and healing. Together with the psychologist, clients develop coping strategies, enhance emotional resilience, and work toward achieving personal goals for mental wellness.
